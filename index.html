Success Stories

Real Impact, Real Lives

Every number represents a transformed life. Meet some of the individuals and families whose journeys exemplify the power of integrated pathway programming.

AM

Amara Mohamed

Complete Pathway Graduate

Amara joined our Foundation Stage at age 8, struggling with literacy and facing food insecurity. Through our integrated approach, she progressed through all three stages over 14 years. Today, she operates a successful tutoring center employing 8 program graduates, serves on our community advisory board, and has helped launch similar programs in 3 neighboring communities. Her business has generated over $280,000 in local economic activity.

14 Years in Program
8 Jobs Created
$280K Economic Impact
RF

The Rahman Family

Multi-Generational Impact

What began with supporting 12-year-old Fatima Rahman's education expanded to include her entire family ecosystem. Her mother completed our adult literacy and microfinance programs, launching a catering business. Her father gained construction skills through our vocational training. Today, three family members are program graduates, two run successful businesses, and they've become community ambassadors who have referred 23 other families to our programs.

3 Family Graduates
23 Families Referred
2 Businesses Launched
RN

Riverside Neighborhood

Community Transformation

Our work in Riverside began with 15 at-risk children. Five years later, the entire neighborhood ecosystem has transformed: 47 families are connected to our programs, 12 community-owned businesses operate in the area, local school test scores have improved by 34%, and a graduate-led community center serves as a hub for continued growth. The neighborhood now has zero youth involved in criminal activity, compared to 23% five years ago.

47 Families Impacted
34% Test Score Increase
0% Youth Crime Rate
